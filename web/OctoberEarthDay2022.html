Election Information

Voter registration forms will be available at our Earth Day event. However, please register sooner to receive your ballots and election info by mail.

Election Day is November 8, 2022. If you mail in your ballot, it must by postmarked November 8 or earlier. You can vote in person or drop your ballot in an official drop box by 8 PM on November 8. In-person early voting is available for 11 days at select locations and for 4 days at the remaining locations.

Register to vote or check your voter registration status at vote.ca.gov. The deadline to register to vote for this election is October 24, 2022.

Ballots will be mailed out to all registered California voters by October 10.

Check your county's Registrar of Voters website for details about where, when, and how to vote. Check if the voting details apply to June or November because some pages on these county sites are still being updated for the November election.

  • Alameda County: acvote.org
    • Stay tuned for information about in-person voting at Alameda County vote centers.
    • 24-hour ballot drop-off is available at Alameda Country ballot drop boxes from October 10 until 8 PM on November 8, 2022 (Election Day).
  • Santa Clara County: sccvote.sccgov.org
    • Stay tuned for information about in-person voting at Santa Clara County vote centers.
    • 24-hour ballot drop-off is available at Santa Clara County ballot drop boxes from October 10 until 8 PM on November 8, 2022 (Election Day).
