Introduction

Citizens' Climate Lobby empowers ordinary people to make their voice heard on climate change. We're the Silicon Valley East local chapter, representing Fremont, Newark, Union City, Milpitas, and surrounding areas.

We meet monthly on the second Saturday at 4 PM. Due to the pandemic, our meetings are all currently virtual. Join us on Meetup for call-in information. We're friendly!
